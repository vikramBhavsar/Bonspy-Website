BYCI-4

Conqueror 1 had explored the ends of systems previously unknown by humans. Now Conqueror 2 sets its sights even further beyond.

As resources on planet Earth run low, our everlasting mission to find more has brought us to the star system known as BYCI. At its core is a G type star, nearly identical to the Sun, after which the system has been named. Five endoplanets revolve around BYCI, two of which fall under the habitable Goldilocks zone. Upon further observation by the space exploration teams at Conqueror, BYCI-4 was found to possess integral traits to support life -

  • the perfect temperature,
  • an abundance of liquid water,
  • and the existence a protective atmosphere.

At CSP, we have been excited to explore the BYCI star system, with a special interest in Planet BYCI-4. Our teams have been working overtime on preparing the Conqueror 2 unmanned mission, which will include the CM-1X, our latest surface rover from the Advanced Robotics Generation.

CSP's Conqueror 2 is set to launch in exactly 3 days from the day this article was published.
